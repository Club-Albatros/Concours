Release Notes for Albatros Concours

When upgrading please make sure you backup your DNN installation. Although a lot care has been taken to make sure the upgrade process is error-free, we cannot guarantee this and DNN has no provision to roll-back partial/broken installations of modules. Failure to take this precaution can leave your installation unusable.

Requirements

  • SQL 2005 or higher
  • DotNetNuke 7.1.2 or higher
  • .Net 3.5+
